Game features

Immersive Storytelling: Dive into a rich narrative that unfolds as you explore the mysterious environments.

Environmental Puzzles: Solve intricate puzzles that are seamlessly integrated into the environment, adding depth to the gameplay and story progression.

Character Progression: Unlock new abilities and tools as you progress, allowing you to explore previously inaccessible areas and uncover hidden secrets.

Multiple Endings: Your choices throughout the game will shape the outcome, leading to multiple endings that reflect the decisions you've made along the way.

Hidden Lore: Discover hidden notes, journals, and artifacts that provide deeper insight into the game world and its history.
